Personal Statement

My path to becoming a licensed nurse as a second-year college student has been gratifying and tough at the same time. Through my experiences, I've come to understand that nursing is a calling rather than merely a career. I know firsthand the value of caring, knowledgeable nurses who can give their patients the very best treatment because I've had many medical operations done in hospitals. My guiding principle as an aspiring professional nurse is the idea that every patient deserves to be treated with respect, sensitivity, and decency. I think a nurse's job description includes advocating for their patients and their families as much as just providing medical care. It's crucial to give patients a secure and pleasant atmosphere while also attending to their specific needs. I hope to go into medicine after nursing school in addition to provide good patient care. My own experiences as a patient have motivated me to pursue a career in medicine so that I may offer a comprehensive approach to patient treatment. I think that working as a nurse will help me to acquire important knowledge and abilities that will better equip me to handle the demands of becoming a doctor. Overall, I've been inspired to seek a career as a professional nurse by my experiences as a patient and my passion for healthcare. I am eager to start this adventure and positively impact the lives of individuals I work with.
